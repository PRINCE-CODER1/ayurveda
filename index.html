The physician

A clinic first.
A shop second.

Vanaushadhi began as a two-room practice above a fabric shop on Lawrence Road. The medicines came later — and only because patients kept asking where to find the same quality outside Amritsar.

“I don't sell anything I wouldn't hand across my own desk. That rule is why the catalogue is short.”

— Dr. H. K. Sandhu

1998 — 2003

Learning the texts, then the patients

BAMS at Guru Ravidas Ayurved University, followed by an MD in Kayachikitsa — internal medicine. Five years spent reading the Charaka Samhita in the morning and running an outpatient ward in the afternoon, learning where the two agreed and where they didn't.

2004

Two rooms on Lawrence Road

The clinic opened with one consulting table, a dispensary cupboard, and eleven patients in the first month. Digestive complaints, joint pain, and sleep — the same three things that still fill half the appointment book.

2011

Going upstream to the growers

Dissatisfied with market-bought churna, Dr. Sandhu began sourcing directly: ashwagandha from Neemuch, tulsi from a family farm outside Kangra, giloy harvested from neem-hosted vines. Single-origin, because potency varies more by soil than by brand.

2016

A GMP kitchen, not a factory

A certified 3,200 sq ft facility in Majitha. Batch sizes stayed deliberately small — 400 to 900 units — so that every lot could be tested for heavy metals, microbial load, and marker compounds before it leaves.

2020 — today

The consulting table, extended

Video consultations opened the practice to 31,000 patients across India. The website exists for one reason: so that the follow-up prescription arrives as reliably as the first one did.

How the medicine thinks

Treat the terrain, not just the symptom.

Ayurveda reads the body as a balance of three functional principles — the doshas. Two people with the same complaint can need opposite medicines. Start by finding out which body you're working with.

Governs circulation, breath, nerve signalling and elimination. When Vata rises you'll notice dry skin, restless sleep, bloating, cold hands and a mind that won't settle. Treatment is warming, oily, grounding and regular — heavy on routine, light on stimulation.
